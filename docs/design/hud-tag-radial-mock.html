HUD tag radial

Long-press a tag — a radial marking menu blooms in the shared HUD material: frosted disc, thin source-bright accent ring, low glow. Four quadrant wedges divided by accent-hairline spokes — Rename, Merge, Delete, Color — each an upright HUD glyph over a sans label; the central hub is the press origin and the cancel target. As you swipe, the options you pass over fade in proportion to how far the cursor rides toward the rim — fill, glyph, and their slice of the frame together: at rest all four hold equal (dial 1); mid-swipe the rest recede to half while your heading brightens (dial 2); near the rim the others all but vanish and the armed wedge takes hold with a brighter wash and an emissive edge flare (dial 3). Delete carries the danger accent. The acted-on tag rides above as its own slanted swatch. Recolor the menu accent with the swatches; the tag keeps its own hue.
